Machine Learning

Machine Learning (ML) is a subset of artificial intelligence (AI) that focuses on developing algorithms and statistical models that enable computers to perform a task without explicit programming. Instead of being explicitly programmed for a particular task, a machine learning model learns from data and improves its performance over time.
